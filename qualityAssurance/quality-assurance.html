Security Measures

For my website, I have applied Ad Hoc Testing to ensure my website is good to go. First of all, I used W3C HTML Validator and W3C CSS Validator to ensure my website meets the language specifications. Second, I have used jshint to ensure there are no errors in my Javascript files. Then I use console.log a lot to make sure my codes are working.At last, I have used both lighthouse auditing and aXe auditing to ensure my codes have met the standard of best coding practices.
